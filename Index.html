Why Candles?


Scented candles have been around for centuries, with the earliest documented use of candles attributed to the Ancient Egyptians. They made torches and candles by soaking reeds in animal fat. Through their experiments, combining spices and extracts, among other aromatic items, they were able to create some of the first-known candles. Today, scented candles are among the most common and most sought-after home fragrance items. Candles have become a signature self-care staple used by many people all across the world.

We invite you to explore our candle selection and take a journey though wooded glens, fields of lavender, and oceanside vistas, all from the comfort of your own home.
